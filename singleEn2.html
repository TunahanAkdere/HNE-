İller Bankası Approved HNE İnşaat's Gölbaşı Sewerage Project


5 July 2023

Click to View the Details



HNE İnşaat has won the Gölbaşı (Adıyaman) Sewage and Potable Water Construction tender, organized by the Iller Bankası with the tender registration number 2023/484594. The tender, conducted through the negotiation method, was approved on May 11, 2023, and the submission of bids took place on May 15, 2023. HNE İnşaat will undertake the project, which has an approximate cost, with a significant discount and will begin construction works. The project will be carried out in Gölbaşı, Adıyaman Province.
