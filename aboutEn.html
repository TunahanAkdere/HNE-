ABOUT US

HNE İNŞAAT

HAKKIMIZDA

There are moments that bring us sweet happiness and light our way... In the freedom of rain falling, the peace of being at home, the sincerity of a heartfelt smile, the aroma of a meal being cooked, or the excitement of taking life's first steps—sometimes, we catch these magical moments on the road to new beginnings and wish they would never end. What makes these moments unforgettable in our lives is experiencing them to the fullest. Since 1979, we’ve been thinking, designing, and bringing to life every detail so that you can enjoy life to the fullest with your loved ones. For 41 years, we’ve been serving as Ateş İnşaat, making sure clean water reaches every place where life exists, rain flows without turning into floods, electricity reaches your homes, natural gas flows to your stoves, and you travel safely on the roads you take. From road construction to electrical systems, we understand the importance of all these fields that represent the quality of a nation’s urbanization and directly affect our societal living standards. That's why we have made it our unwavering goal to do the best for our country. As the invisible heroes of your precious moments, we are doing everything we can to raise the standard of living for you and our nation. To date, we have successfully completed many projects both domestically and internationally. With our new projects, we continue our work with great care. As Ateş İnşaat, we are proudly carrying out infrastructure and road projects for the Ankara Space and Aviation Specialized Organized Industrial Zone, which covers an area of 7.2 million square meters and will be the center of local and national production in the defense industry. Since 1979, on this journey for a strong and great Türkiye, we continue to serve all corners of our country in the fields of construction and infrastructure with our professional team, never compromising on quality. As we continue on this path to support the prosperity of our ever-growing and developing country, we carry with great care the rightful pride of being the best in our field, always striving to be worthy of our nation. For the peace, security, and prosperity of our country, we strive for the very best together!
